3 COURSES TO SUCCESS

Having taught literacy for over 20 years, we have shaped an innovative method that works for anyone. We have three simplified courses to reflect our belief that literacy is both scientific and artistic. We start by opening a student's mind to the wonderful world of language. Then, we move into a stage of conversation, communication, and exploring all the possibilities literacy allows. Lastly, we refine interactions and teach the subtle art for those who desire to become master poets and have their words invoke action and emotion within others.

Learn the LINGO
Speak like a Local

Don't bite off more than you can chew. What's up? The ball's in your court! Don't judge a book by its cover. Don't bark up the wrong tree. Actions speak louder than words. How you doin?

These phrases are known as lingo and make the difference between you sounding like a foreigner and being embraced by the American culture. Our program is designed by an American author who has taught Storytelling to American students for many years.
